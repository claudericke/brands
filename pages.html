PAGES GOVERNANCE POLICY

Community standards

Brandsapp gives business and enterprises around the world the power to publish their own products and services, see the world through the eyes of their many customers and potential customers, and connect and share wherever they go. The interaction that happens on brandsapp and opinions expressed mirror the diversity of the users using Brandsapp.

To balance the needs and interests of the global population, Brandsapp protects expression that meets the community standards outlined on this page. Please review these standards. They will help you understand what type of expression is acceptable, and what type of content may be reported and removed.

Violence and threats

Safety is Brandsapp top priority. We remove content and may escalate to low enforcement when we perceive a genuine risk of physical harm, or a direct threat to public safety. You may not credibly threaten other competitors or organise acts of real world violence. Organisations with a record of terrorists or violent criminal activities are not allowed to maintain a presence on our site. You will not post unauthorized commercial communications (such as spam) on Brands. You will not collect user’s contents or information or otherwise access the Brands app, using automated means (such as harvesting bots, robots, spiders, scrapers) without our prior permission.

Self-harm

Brandsapp takes threats of self-harm very seriously. We remove any promotion or encouragement of self mutilation, eating disorders or hard drug abuse. We also work with suicide prevention agencies around the world to provide assistance for people in distress. You will not engage in unlawful multi-level marketing, such as pyramid scheme, on the Brands app.

Bullying and harassment

Brandsapp does tolerate bullying or harassment. We allow users to speak freely on matters and people and businesses of public interests, par-take action on all reports of abusive behaviour directed at private individuals. Repeatedly targeting other competitors with unwanted bi-laws (business pricing, policy, marketing integrating and any other thereof) is a form of harassment. You will not bully, intimidate or harass any user.

Hate speech

Brandspp does not permit hate speech, but distinguishes between serious and humorous speech. While we encourage you to challenge ideas, institutions, events, practices, we do not permit individuals/users or groups to attack others based on their race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, sex, gender, sexual orientation, disability or medical condition. You will not post content that is hate speech, threatening or pornographic incites violence; or contains nudity or graphic or gratuitous violence.

Graphic content

Brandsapp is going to be a place where people/users tend to share their experiences and raise awareness pertaining to products and services important to them. Sometimes, those experiences and issues involve graphic content that is of public interest or concern, such as human rights abuses or acts of terrorism. In many instances, when people share this type of content, it is to condemn it. However, graphic images shared for sadistic effect or to celebrate or glorify violence have no place on our site. When people/users share any content, we expect that they will share in responsible manner. That includes choosing carefully the audience for the content. For graphic videos, businesses/enterprises should warn their audience about nature of content in the video so that their audience can make an informed choice about whether to watch it.

Nudity and pornography

Brandsapp has a strict policy against the sharing of pornographic content and any explicitly sexual content were a minor is involved or any party thereof. We also impose limitation on the display of nudity. We aspire to respect businesses and people’s rights to share content of personal importance whether those are photos of a sculpture like Michlangelo’s David family photos of a child breastfeeding.

Identity and privacy

On Brandsapp people connect using their real names and identities. (Registration of company information should be and will be registered likewise). We may ask for more additional information on identity and a 48hr response from such a time such a request has been made by us. We ask that you refrain from publishing the personal information of others without their consent. Claiming to be another person or company or enterprise, creating a false presence for an organisation or creating multiple accounts undermines community and violates Brandsapp terms. You will not share your password (or incase of developers, your secret key, let anyone else access your account or do anything else that may/might jeopardize the security of your account. You will not transfer your account (including any page or application you administer) to anyone without getting our written permission.

Intellectual property

Before sharing content on Brandsapp, please be sure you have the right to do so. We ask that you respect copyrights, trademarks and other legal rights.

Regulated goods

It is not permitted to complete transactions involving regulated goods on our platform. If you post a product involving fire arms, alcohol, tobacco or adult products, we expect you to make sure you following all applicable laws and consider carefully the audience for that content. If you are using a page to connect with your customers and other audiences, you need to abide by our pages terms.

Phishing and spam

We take the safety of our members seriously and work to prevent attempts to compromise their privacy or security. We also ask that you respect our members by not contacting them for commercial purposes without their consent. You will not post unauthorized commercial communications (such as spam) on Brands.

Security

We take the safety of our members seriously and work to prevent to compromise their privacy or security, including those that use fraud or deception. Additionally, we ask that you respect our members by not contacting them for commercial purposes without their consent.

Reporting abuse

If you see something on Brandsapp that you believe violates our terms, you should report it to us. Please keep in mind that reporting a piece of content does not guarantee that it will be removed from the site.

Because of the diversity of our community, it is possible that something could be disagreeable or disturbing to you without meeting the criteria for being removed or blocked. For this reason, we also offer personal controls over what you see, such as the ability to hide or quietly cut ties with people, pages, or applications that offend you.
