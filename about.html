ABOUT BRANDS

Brands is an application specifically made for mobile smart phones, pc, tablets and desktops. Brands is an application that provides organizations with a platform to communicate with the public (clients, potential clients, customers, potential customers). Organizations place all their information open to the public on this platform and the public can easily access information about a company of their choice. Each company will have its own account and on their homepage they will be a number of fields which include products/ services, prices, enquiries, promotions, suggestions, complaints, job vacancies and other various more fields.

The application is a commercial platform that brings organizations closely to the public at large and is also a marketing tool. It accesses all this information from the database through the server, which means information is pre-loaded. Information on the application database can be accessed from two types of accounts:-

1) Company Accounts
2) Public Accounts
